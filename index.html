Recommendations

7 prioritized actions based on Jan 1 – Jan 31, 2026 performance · all figures SAR

1

Scale Google Display — Best CPC at SAR 0.04

Google Display delivers 1.6M clicks at SAR 0.04 CPC — 2.5× cheaper than any other channel. Increase daily budget by 30–50% while efficiency holds. Add UTM parameters to validate click quality via GA4.

Google
2

Shift 20–30% of IG Budget to Facebook

Meta FB CPC (SAR 0.12) is 5× cheaper than IG (SAR 0.63). FB also has 4× the CTR. Unless IG shows superior downstream conversion, move ~SAR 10,000 from IG to FB to increase click volume at no added spend.

Meta
3

Prioritize TikTok UGC

UGC achieves 61% more video completions (109,761 vs 68,061), better reach (5.85M vs 4.07M), and lower frequency (8.22x vs 10.68x) for only 14% more spend. Shift 60% of TikTok budget to UGC and refresh creatives monthly.

TikTok
4

Fix Frequency Fatigue — Snap, TikTok, Meta FB

All three exceed 9x frequency. Actions: (a) broaden audience targeting, (b) introduce minimum 3 creative variants per channel, (c) set frequency caps at 7–8x per campaign flight immediately.

All Channels
5

Use Snap's 2.22% CTR for Retargeting Layer

Snap's swipe rate is the strongest social signal. Build a retargeting audience from swipe-engagers who didn't convert. A dedicated lower-funnel creative with a clear CTA will make Snap's SAR 11.78 CPM more justifiable through higher intent.

Snapchat
6

Grow Google Search — 11.56% CTR Is Under-Funded

Search spend (SAR 42,994) is less than Display (SAR 61,407) despite delivering higher-intent users at SAR 0.53 CPC. Recommend shifting SAR 10,000 from Display and expanding branded + product keyword coverage.

Google
7

Implement Cross-Channel Conversion Tracking

Zero conversion data exists across all four channels. Set up consistent UTM parameters, connect to GA4 or a CDP, and define at least one conversion event (app install, card application, form fill) to measure true ROAS and channel contribution.

All Channels
